Delete Connection

Deleting a connection permanently removes a connection from the system and the database.

Assumptions/Prerequisites

In order to delete a connection, you must first:

  1. Create a connection

Instructions

  1. Click the "Delete Connection" icon on main page next to the connection you want to remove from the system.

  2. You are presented with a popup and are asked if you are sure that you want remove the connection.
  3. Click the "Delete" button.

Warnings

  • The only warning you are presented with is the popup asking if you are sure about deleting the connection selected.

Notes

  • Deleting a connection removes it from the local database and the system.
