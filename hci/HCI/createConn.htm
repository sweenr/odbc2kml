Create Connection

Creating a connection is the only way to connect to a ODBC database. This process is the basis of our entire project and is one of the more important features.

Assumptions/Prerequirements

The only assumption in creating a connection is that the ODBC database exists and that your connection information is correct.

Instructions

  1. Click the button "New Connection" on main page.

  2. Fill out the following: Connection Name, Database Address, Port Number, Database Name, Username, Password, and Database Type.
    If your "Database Type" is Oracle you will be presented with Protocol, Service Name, and Service ID.

  3. Click the button "Create" on the "Connection Information" window.

Warnings

  • When filling out the "Connection Information" window if any of the information is incorrect or not filled out when you click the "Create" button you will receive the appropriate error message.

Notes

  • Currently standard port numbers are the only ones tested.
  • If your "Database Type" is Oracle you only need to fill out the Protocol and Service ID or Service Name not both.
